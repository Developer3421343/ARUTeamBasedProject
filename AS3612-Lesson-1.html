Probability

Probability is the branch of mathematics that deals with measuring and analyzing uncertainty. It provides a framework for quantifying how likely an event is to occur, expressed as a number between 0 (impossible) and 1 (certain). For example, flipping a fair coin gives a probability of 0.5 for landing heads and 0.5 for tails.
