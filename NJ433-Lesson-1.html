Prime Numbers

Definition

A Prime Number is a whole number that is greater than 1, that has exactly two factors 1 and the number it self.

Key points to remember

  1. It must be a whole number
  2. Must be greater than 1
  3. Only two divisors.

Examples

Number Factors Prime?
2 1,2 Yes
3 1,3 Yes
4 1,2,4 No
7 1,7 Yes
